At ELECTRON we want to solve the biggest problem in Electric vehicles.

In making India a pollution-free country by 2030 India has to completely drop its dependency on ICEVs (Internal Combustion Engine Vehicles) which use fossil fuels and release harmful toxic gases like carbon monoxide (CO) and nitrogen oxide (NOX) and many more gases. Shifting to EVs (Electric Vehicles) will be a much better option in reducing air pollution and making India a healthy country to live. If India wants to achieve their goal of all-electric vehicles on road by 2030 India have to build a strong backbone for its charging at every possible spot. Because the charger is an essential part of EVs, it should be presented everywhere like Gasoline pumps. The future of Electric vehicles (EVs) is completely dependent on charging stations (CS). Charging Infrastructure will play a pivotal role in EV deployment. Apart from the charge that will be used in homes for charging personal vehicles, India needs to work on more charging stations at workplaces, parking towers, malls, hotels, etc. to make this process fast I created a website.
